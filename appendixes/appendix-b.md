# APPENDIX B — STRUCTURAL EXCLUSIONS & ORPHANED POSITIONS

Navigation: [Previous Appendix](appendix-a.md) | [Contents](../contents.md) | [Methodology](../methodology.md) | [Next Appendix](appendix-c.md)

Documented honestly: real achievements no work-unit can carry.

**No work-unit exists:** the alphabet · Babylonian mathematics and astronomy · the lexical lists (Listenwissenschaft) · khipu · rongorongo (undeciphered) · thermodynamics (no single summit unit) · Mendel (embedded paper) · Leonardo (unpublished) · Peirce · Hypatia (nothing survives) · Mani (scriptures lost) · Pythagoras and Mādhava (founders without texts).

**Living traditions without definitive form:** Gesar · Manas · Jangar · the Ifa (Odu) corpus · I La Galigo · the Nart sagas · Diné Bahaneʼ (with sacred-restriction note) · the Great Law of Peace (mediated reconstructions; flag closed, exclusion final).

**Orphaned positions on record:** Yangism · the sophists (near-total loss) · euhemerism · Zurvanism · Abū Bakr al-Rāzī's freethought (carried forward by al-Maʿarrī) · Catharism (anticosmism carried by the Apocryphon of John) · Latin Averroism.
